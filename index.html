What's Here

Quenington Wildlife Park hosts a wide variety of animals and birds. We have a large scenic forest and a beautiful lake. We are open everyday from spring to autumn.

Cafeteria

There is a cafe here serving hot tea, coffee and other refreshments. We have seats and tables indoors as well as outdoors.
